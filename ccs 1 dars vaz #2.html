Lesson 1: Surveying the Possibilities

Overview

The purpose of this lesson is to begin considering what makes a high-quality
website.As a group,we will explore questions such as these:
  • Why do you feel that some websites are good?

  • Why do you feel that some websites are not so good?

  • Do others agree with your opinion?

  • Who is target audience for the particular size?

  • How might website quality be judjed differently across differen audiences?

  • Are there some qualities of websites that all audiences would agree are
    • good?What are these qualities?

Learner Outcomes

At the completion of this exercise:

  • you will be able to critically evaluate web sites based on purpose,design
    • and usability.

  • you will be able to identify the major criteria that experts use to evaluate web
    • sites and apply those criteria to your own evaluations.
